Welcome to Tallearn

Tallearn is more than learning words and phrases – it is a chance to explore interesting ideas, discover other cultures, and develop new communication skills that will take you around the world. We are grateful to have you as our students!

Why choose us?

– A huge number of successful interviews. Our students get into cool IT companies the first time! I know how to win over an HR manager and successfully pass an interview in English.

– A greate number of emigrated students. The USA, Poland, Great Britain, Lithuania are among the dozens of countries where my students have moved. With me, you will speak real English and become a full-fledged member of the society of the desired country.

What we do?

We provide :

  • Excellent language teaching and good atmosphere in the classroom;
  • Individual selection of the group according to the level and schedule;
  • Small groups (3-5 people in a group);
  • Interesting communication programs and classes that develop all aspects of the language;
  • Full provision of our students with textbooks and materials;
  • Individual approach to the teaching of students.

Available courses

Learn English, German or Russian. Whether you are looking for a short intensive language course or year-long overseas adventure we have a language program to suit you.

Experienced Teachers

Certified teachers with high education and teaching experience of 5+ years

Professional Courses

Online/Offline English, German and Russian language courses with individual and professional approach to every student

Communicative approach

Our teaching methods are based on a communicative and lexical approach, because a foreign language is, first of all, a means of communication

Study and have fun

In the classroom we focus on spoken English and together with students select relevant topics. The material is never boring but is remembered easily and quickly. For students we try to be not only teachers but friends. We are eager to help you to believe in your strength and charge you with optimism on the way to knowledge!
